--- layout: post title: IronPython 2.7.4 Release Candidate Available date: '2013-08-17T19:02:00.001-07:00' author: jdhardy tags: - Release Announcements modified_time: '2013-08-17T19:02:40.335-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-9121756179884119374.post-2067045878085499251 blogger_orig_url: http://blog.ironpython.net/2013/08/ironpython-274-release-candidate.html --- The first (and hopefully only) release candidate of IronPython 2.7.4 is now available. If there are no major issues this will become the final release around August 31.

Changes since the last beta include an updated C#-SQLite binding and a bunch of fixes that were made to the master branch long ago but never got cross-ported to the 2.7 maintenance branch.
